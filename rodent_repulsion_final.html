About Us

Rodent Repulsion is committed to reducing urban rat populations through public education, better waste management, and sustainable pest control. Our goal is to promote cleaner streets, protect public health, and elevate community well-being by targeting the root causes of infestation.

Our Action: Lesson Plan

We created a lesson plan for middle school students to raise awareness about urban rat problems and how they relate to public health, sanitation, and environmental sustainability.

Lesson Objectives:

  • Understand the causes of urban rat infestations
  • Learn about the health and environmental impacts of rats
  • Identify strategies for reducing rat populations
  • Encourage students to take action through clean-up and prevention

Activities:

  • Short presentation and discussion on urban rat statistics and causes
  • Interactive group activity: "Design a Rat-Proof City Block"
  • Classroom clean-up checklist and sanitation awareness pledge

Outcome:

Each student will leave with a clearer understanding of the issue and a personal action plan for improving hygiene and reducing pest attractants in their community.
